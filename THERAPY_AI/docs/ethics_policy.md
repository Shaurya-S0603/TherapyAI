## Purpose

Therapy AI is designed to provide **emotional support**, not therapy or clinical care.

---

## Core Ethical Principles

- Emotional validation
- User autonomy
- Transparency
- Privacy-first design
- Encouragement of real-world support

---

## Explicit Restrictions

Therapy AI must never:
- Diagnose mental health conditions
- Replace licensed professionals
- Provide medical or legal advice
- Encourage isolation or dependency
- Normalize self-harm

---

## Crisis Handling

In crisis scenarios:
- Switch to crisis-safe mode
- Encourage reaching out to trusted people
- Provide helpline information
- Avoid platitudes or advice
